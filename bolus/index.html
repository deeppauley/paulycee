Bolus is a 25-year veteran DJ and maven of the electronic dance music scene. Staying true to the spirit of Balearic beat, his curiously eclectic sound moves tastefully from deep house to straight techno.

Hypnotically percussive and driven by rhythmic bass lines and tribal elements, his sets are occasionally minimal, often deep, and always built for the dance floor—intelligent dance music for clubbers with a rich palate.
